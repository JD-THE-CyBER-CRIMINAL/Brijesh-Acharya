ABOUT SERVICES

Vedic Gyan Vigyan

Vedic gyan vigyan refers to the knowledge and science derived from the ancient Vedic texts of India. It encompasses a profound understanding of spiritual wisdom and scientific principles embedded in these scriptures, exploring both metaphysical concepts and practical knowledge relevant to various aspects of life and the universe.





Navgrah Puja

Navgraha puja is a Hindu ritual worshiping the nine celestial bodies or planets, seeking their blessings and appeasing any negative influences. It involves chanting mantras, offering prayers, and performing rituals to honor each planet, aiming to bring harmony and mitigate astrological challenges in one's life.




Havan/Yajna

Havan or yajna is a sacred Vedic ritual in Hinduism where offerings such as ghee, grains, and herbs are ceremonially offered into a sacred fire. It is performed to invoke specific deities, seek blessings, purify the environment, and promote spiritual growth and well-being through the divine energy generated by the fire.




Satya Narayan Katha

Satya Narayan Katha is a Hindu religious narrative dedicated to Lord Vishnu in his form as Satya Narayan. It recounts the story of a virtuous king and the miraculous events that unfold due to his devotion. Devotees perform this ritualistic storytelling with prayers and offerings, seeking blessings for prosperity, peace, and fulfillment of wishes.




LORD OF THE UNIVERSE

Brahma, Vishnu, and Mahesh (Shiva) form the triumvirate of major deities in Hinduism. Brahma is the creator, Vishnu the preserver, and Shiva the destroyer or transformer. Together, they represent the cycle of creation, preservation, and dissolution in the universe, embodying different aspects of cosmic existence and balance.



BRAHMA JI

Brahma Ji, in Hindu mythology, is the creator deity, symbolizing creation and the unfolding of the universe from his divine essence

VISHNU JI

Vishnu Ji, a principal deity in Hinduism, is revered as the preserver of the universe, embodying mercy, goodness, and protection for all beings

SHIV JI

Shiv Ji is the Hindu deity of destruction and transformation, embodying the cycles of creation and dissolution.
